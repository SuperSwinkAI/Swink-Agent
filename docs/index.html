Your tireless agent loop, in pure Rust.

No unsafe. No globals. #[forbid(unsafe_code)] at every crate root. Pure, predictable state.
Policy guardrails, not afterthoughts Budget caps, sandboxing, PII redaction, prompt injection detection — compiled into the loop, not bolted on. Zero latency. No extra LLM calls.
Batteries included 9 LLM adapters, session memory, eval harness, on-device inference, and a full TUI — all in the workspace.
